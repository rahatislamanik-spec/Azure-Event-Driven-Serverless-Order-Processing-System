Phase 4
validate_order — Queue Trigger, Fan-Out, and the Base64 Bug
validate_order deployed as a queue-triggered function. Documents the major debugging session: every message silently moved to poison queue until the root cause was found — Azure Functions queue trigger expected Base64, Storage SDK sent plain JSON. Fixed with messageEncoding: none in host.json.
